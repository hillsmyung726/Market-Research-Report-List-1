<p><strong>The Automotive Triaxial Gyroscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Interferometric Gyroscope</li><li>Resonant Gyroscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive triaxial gyroscope market includes various types, primarily the interferometric gyroscope and the resonant gyroscope. Interferometric gyroscopes use laser beams to detect changes in rotation, offering high precision and stability, making them suitable for advanced navigation systems. Resonant gyroscopes, on the other hand, rely on the principles of resonance to measure angular motion, offering a compact and cost-effective solution for automotive applications. Both types are essential for improving vehicle stability, navigation, and overall safety.</p></p>
