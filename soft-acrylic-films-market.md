<p><p>The soft acrylic films market is segmented into single-side coated and double-side coated types. Single-side coated films feature a protective or adhesive layer on only one side, making them ideal for applications requiring one-sided adhesion or protection. In contrast, double-side coated films have an acrylic coating on both sides, offering enhanced versatility for bonding, lamination, and protection. These products are widely used in industries like packaging, automotive, and electronics due to their durability and optical clarity.</p></p>
