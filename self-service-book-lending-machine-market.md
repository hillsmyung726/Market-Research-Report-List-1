<p><p>The Self-service Book Lending Machine market features two primary types of touch screens: infrared and capacitive. Infrared touch screens use light beams to detect touch, providing a cost-effective and durable option, ideal for environments with heavy use. Capacitive touch screens, using the electrical properties of the human body, offer a more responsive and precise user experience, making them suitable for more modern, sleek designs. Both types enhance user interaction and streamline the book lending process in libraries and similar settings.</p></p>
