<p><p>The Youth Goggles market is categorized into two main types: absorption-type and reflection-type. Absorption-type goggles utilize materials that absorb harmful UV rays and reduce glare, offering clear visibility and eye protection during outdoor activities. In contrast, reflection-type goggles feature mirrored lenses that reflect sunlight, providing a stylish look while enhancing visibility and reducing eye strain. Both types cater to the needs of young individuals engaged in sports and outdoor adventures, promoting eye safety and comfort.</p></p>
