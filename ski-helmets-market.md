<p><p>The ski helmet market primarily comprises two types of shell materials: PC (Polycarbonate) and ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene). PC shells are known for their lightweight and high-impact resistance, offering better durability and safety during falls. They typically feature a sleek design and enhanced ventilation for comfort. Conversely, ABS shells tend to be heavier but provide excellent impact resistance and are often more affordable. Both types aim to enhance skier safety while providing varied options to meet consumer preferences.</p></p>
