<p><p>The Military Electronic Chart Display and Information System (ECDIS) market is crucial for enhancing naval navigation and operational efficiency. Within this market, Electronic Navigational Charts (ENC) provide vector-based, up-to-date, and digitally structured maritime data, enabling precise navigation. In contrast, Raster Navigational Charts (RNC) offer scanned images of traditional paper charts, ensuring familiarity for mariners while transitioning to digital systems. Both types serve to improve situational awareness and decision-making during military maritime operations, promoting safety and effectiveness at sea.</p></p>
