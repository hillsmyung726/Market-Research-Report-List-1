<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Gypsum Mining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anhydrite Ore Deposits</li><li>Fibrous Gypsum Deposits</li><li>Common Gypsum Deposits</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gypsum mining involves extracting various types of gypsum deposits, each with distinct characteristics. Anhydrite ore deposits consist of anhydrous calcium sulfate, often found in deep geological formations. Fibrous gypsum deposits contain needle-like crystals, prized for their strength and used in specialized applications. Common gypsum deposits are the most widespread, composed of hydrated calcium sulfate, and are primarily used in construction and agriculture. Other markets include rare or unique gypsum varieties, catering to niche industrial or artistic uses, expanding the mineral's versatility.</p></p>
