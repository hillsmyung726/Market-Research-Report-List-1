<p><strong>The Portable Handheld VOC Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable handheld VOC monitor market is primarily categorized into two main types: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID monitors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds, providing highly sensitive detection across a wide range of VOCs, making them ideal for environmental monitoring and industrial applications. In contrast, MOS sensors detect VOCs through changes in electrical resistance as gases interact with a heated semiconductor material, offering a cost-effective solution for basic air quality assessments. Both technologies serve crucial roles in ensuring safety and compliance.</p></p>
