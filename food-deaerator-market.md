<p><p>The food deaerator market comprises three main types: spray-tray type, vacuum type, and spray type. The spray-tray type utilizes a combination of spraying liquid and gravitational flow through trays to remove dissolved gases. The vacuum type operates under reduced pressure, efficiently extracting air and preserving product quality. The spray type focuses on atomizing liquid to enhance gas removal, making it suitable for various food applications. Each type serves specific needs in the food processing industry, enhancing product shelf life and quality.</p></p>
