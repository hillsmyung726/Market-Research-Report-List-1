<p><ul><li>Rotatable Transformation</li><li>Non Rotating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnesium oxide sputtering target market is divided into two primary types: rotatable transformation and non-rotating type. Rotatable transformation targets enable continuous deposition by allowing the target to spin, ensuring uniform material application and extended target lifetime. In contrast, non-rotating targets are stationary and typically utilized for shorter production runs or specific applications where precision and cost-effectiveness are prioritized. The choice between these types depends on production requirements, desired film quality, and operational efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
