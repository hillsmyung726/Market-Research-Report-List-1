<p><strong>The Infrared Hydrocarbon Gas Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point IR Detectors</li><li>Open Path IR Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared hydrocarbon gas detector market is primarily categorized into two types: point IR detectors and open path IR detectors. Point IR detectors are designed to locate and measure gas concentrations at specific points, often used in confined spaces for accurate detection. In contrast, open path IR detectors continuously monitor larger areas by analyzing the infrared light absorption over a predetermined path, making them effective for detecting gas leaks across broad environments. Both technologies enhance safety and environmental compliance in various industries.</p></p>
