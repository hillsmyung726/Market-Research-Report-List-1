<p><p>The Cross-Laminated Timber (CLT) market comprises two primary types: adhesive-bonded CLT and mechanically fastened CLT. Adhesive-bonded CLT utilizes strong adhesives to bond layers of wood at specific angles, providing enhanced structural integrity and stability. In contrast, mechanically fastened CLT incorporates screws or nails to connect layers, allowing for faster assembly and ease in disassembly. Both types offer unique benefits, such as sustainability and energy efficiency, catering to diverse construction needs and preferences in the building industry.</p></p>
