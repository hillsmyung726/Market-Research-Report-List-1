<p><p>Automated fluid dispensing systems streamline the precise dispensing of liquids in various industries. The market is primarily categorized into three types: valves, pumps, and dispensers. Valves control the flow and direction of fluids, ensuring accurate delivery. Pumps are responsible for moving liquids from one place to another, offering consistent pressure and flow rates. Dispensers combine both functions, allowing for user-friendly, automated portioning of fluids. Together, these components enhance efficiency, reduce waste, and improve overall process control in manufacturing and packaging applications.</p></p>
