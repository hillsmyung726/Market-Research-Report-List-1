<p><p>The transmitting antennas market is categorized by frequency bands, including HF (High Frequency), VHF (Very High Frequency), and UHF (Ultra High Frequency). HF antennas operate between 3 MHz to 30 MHz, primarily used for long-distance communication, especially in maritime and amateur radio. VHF antennas, ranging from 30 MHz to 300 MHz, are commonly used in television broadcasting, two-way radios, and public safety communications. UHF antennas, which span from 300 MHz to 3 GHz, are essential for modern wireless communication, including mobile networks and Wi-Fi. Each frequency band serves distinct applications, enhancing communication capabilities across various sectors.</p></p>
