<p><strong>The Smoke Exhaust Ventilators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side Suction</li><li>Top Suction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smoke exhaust ventilators are critical for fire safety, aiding in the removal of smoke and heat from buildings during emergencies. The market is primarily divided into two types: side suction and top suction ventilators. Side suction ventilators draw smoke horizontally from the sides, making them suitable for low-rise structures. In contrast, top suction ventilators operate by removing smoke vertically from the top, ideal for high-rise buildings. Both designs enhance safety and improve visibility during fire incidents, ensuring effective smoke management.</p></p>
