<p><p>The Vehicle HVAC market is categorized into Independent Type and Not Independent Type systems. Independent Type HVAC systems operate separately from the vehicle's engine, often using electric or auxiliary power, making them energy-efficient and suitable for electric or hybrid vehicles. Not Independent Type systems rely on the vehicle's engine for power, commonly found in traditional internal combustion engine vehicles. Both types aim to regulate cabin temperature, but their power sources and applications differ based on vehicle design and energy efficiency needs.</p></p>
