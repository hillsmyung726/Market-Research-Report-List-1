<p><p>Infrared (IR) heat lamps are categorized into three main types based on their bulb design: Red Type, Clear Type, and Inside Frosted Type. Red Type lamps emit a deep red glow, ideal for therapeutic and heating applications. Clear Type lamps produce bright, unfiltered light, suitable for industrial and commercial heating. Inside Frosted Type lamps have a frosted interior, diffusing light evenly and reducing glare, making them perfect for residential and ambient heating. Each type serves specific purposes across industries.</p></p>
