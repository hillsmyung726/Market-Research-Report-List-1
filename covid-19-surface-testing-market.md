<p><strong>The COVID-19 Surface Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ATP Swab Testing</li><li>PCR or Genetic Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 surface testing market primarily comprises ATP swab testing and PCR or genetic testing. ATP swab testing measures adenosine triphosphate levels to evaluate surface cleanliness and potential contamination, indicating microbial presence. In contrast, PCR testing identifies the genetic material of the virus on surfaces, providing a more precise detection of active infections. Both methods serve crucial roles in monitoring and controlling the spread of COVID-19 in environments such as healthcare facilities and public spaces.</p></p>
