<p><p>The genset battery market primarily includes Lead-Acid and Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd) batteries. Lead-Acid batteries are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness, high reliability, and ability to deliver substantial power for short durations, making them suitable for backup generators. NiCd batteries, while more expensive, offer longer life cycles, better performance in extreme temperatures, and rapid recharging capabilities. Their durability and efficiency make them ideal for applications requiring consistent power supply in critical situations, enhancing overall generator performance.</p></p>
