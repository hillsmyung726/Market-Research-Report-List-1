<p><p>The roof extractor fan market comprises various types, primarily including centrifugal and axial fans. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to increase air pressure and efficiently move air through ducts, making them ideal for high-resistance systems. Axial fans, on the other hand, propel air parallel to the fan's axis, providing high airflow with lower pressure, suitable for open spaces. Other types may include mixed-flow and propulsion fans, catering to specific ventilation needs in industrial, commercial, and residential applications.</p></p>
