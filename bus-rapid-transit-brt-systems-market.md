The **Semi-Open Exclusive Lane** restricts access to specific vehicles like emergency services or authorized transport, balancing exclusivity and shared use. **Other market types** include hybrid or mixed systems, adapting to unique urban needs, such as partial dedicated lanes or flexible routing, enhancing connectivity and reducing congestion in diverse city environments.</p></p>
