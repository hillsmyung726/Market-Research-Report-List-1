<p><p>In the Spring Loaded Pressure Safety Valve (PSV) market, there are two primary types: closed type and unclosed type. Closed type PSVs are sealed off from the atmosphere and operate under specific pressure conditions, ensuring safety without atmospheric exposure. In contrast, unclosed type PSVs are vented to the atmosphere, allowing for discharge during overpressure situations. Both types serve crucial roles in maintaining system safety, though their operational environments and applications vary, depending on regulatory and safety requirements.</p></p>
