<p><ul><li>Rolling Bearing</li><li>Plain Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear motion bearing market is segmented into two main types: rolling bearings and plain bearings. Rolling bearings utilize balls or rollers to reduce friction and enable smooth linear movement, making them suitable for high-speed applications with precise positioning. In contrast, plain bearings rely on sliding surfaces without rolling elements, providing cost-effective solutions for low-speed, high-load scenarios. Both types offer distinct advantages, catering to diverse industrial needs in automation, machinery, and transportation industries, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
