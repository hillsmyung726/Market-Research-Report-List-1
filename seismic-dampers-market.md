<p><p>The seismic dampers market consists of various types designed to mitigate earthquake forces on structures. Viscous dampers dissipate energy through fluid flow, while friction dampers absorb energy via surfaces sliding against each other. Yield dampers deform under stress to absorb energy, while magnetic dampers utilize magnetic fields for energy dissipation. Tuned mass dampers counteract vibrations by using a mass-spring system tuned to specific frequencies. Other dampers encompass innovative solutions that enhance structural integrity during seismic events.</p></p>
