Instrumented Systems (SIS) ensure safety and process integrity by automatically managing hazardous scenarios. Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC) offer flexible, programmable solutions for automating repetitive tasks, ensuring streamlined operations and improved reliability in CHP facilities.</p></p>
