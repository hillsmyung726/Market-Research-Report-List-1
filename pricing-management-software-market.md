<p><p>Pricing management software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, ease of updates, and reduced IT overhead. It is ideal for businesses looking for flexibility and lower upfront costs. Conversely, on-premises software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control, customization, and security. However, it typically requires significant initial investment and ongoing maintenance, making it suitable for firms needing robust data protection.</p></p>
