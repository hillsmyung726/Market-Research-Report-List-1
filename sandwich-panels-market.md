<p><p>The sandwich panels market comprises several types characterized by their insulation materials and fire ratings. PIR Bs1 and PIR Bs2 panels feature polyisocyanurate, offering high thermal efficiency and varying fire resistance. PUR Bs2 panels, made from polyurethane, also provide excellent insulation but differ in fire classification. PUR Cs3 panels are similar but with lower fire ratings, while PUR F panels excel in fire resistance. EPS sandwich panels utilize expanded polystyrene for cost-effective insulation, while mineral wool panels offer superior fire resistance and sound insulation, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
