<p><p>The high-efficiency solar modules market encompasses advanced technologies such as Heterojunction (HJT) cells and Interdigitated Back Contact (IBC) cells. HJT cells combine crystalline silicon with amorphous silicon layers, enhancing efficiency and reducing degradation. IBC cells, featuring contacts on the rear side, maximize light absorption and reduce shading losses. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for sustainable energy solutions, offering superior performance and efficiency compared to traditional solar modules, making them increasingly popular in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
