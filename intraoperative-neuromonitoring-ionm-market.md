Insource IONM refers to facilities that employ their own staff and equipment to provide monitoring services during surgery, ensuring direct control over operations. In contrast, Outsource IONM involves partnering with external specialized companies for monitoring services, allowing facilities to leverage specialized expertise without managing in-house resources.</p></p>
