<p><p>The smoke alarm market is primarily categorized into three types: Photoelectric smoke alarms, which utilize a light source to detect smoke, making them effective for smoldering fires; Ionization smoke alarms, which use radioactive materials to sense fast-flaming fires, offering quick response to blaze-like flames; and Combination smoke alarms, integrating both photoelectric and ionization technologies for comprehensive detection. Each type serves distinct fire detection needs, influencing market trends based on safety regulations, consumer preferences, and technological advancements.</p></p>
