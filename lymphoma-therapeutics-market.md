<p><p>The lymphoma therapeutics market comprises several key types of treatment modalities. Chemotherapy involves the use of cytotoxic drugs that kill rapidly dividing cells but often with significant side effects. Immunotherapy harnesses the body's immune system to fight lymphoma, offering newer approaches like CAR-T cell therapy. The market also includes approved drugs and pipeline drugs, indicating ongoing research and development for innovative therapies.</p></p>
