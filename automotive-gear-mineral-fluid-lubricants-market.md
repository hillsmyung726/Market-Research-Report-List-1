<p><p>The automotive gear mineral fluid lubricants market is categorized into three types: paraffinic, naphthenic, and aromatic lubricants. Paraffinic lubricants are derived from saturated hydrocarbons, known for their stability and resistance to oxidation. Naphthenic lubricants contain cyclic hydrocarbons, offering excellent thermal stability and low pour points, making them suitable for extreme conditions. Aromatic lubricants, characterized by their aromatic hydrocarbon content, provide good solvency and can enhance the performance of certain additives. Each type has specific applications based on performance requirements.</p></p>
