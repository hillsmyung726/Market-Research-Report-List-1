<p><p>The underfloor heating systems market is primarily categorized into gas heating and electric heating systems. Gas heating utilizes hot water circulated through pipes, providing efficient, even warmth and is often connected to a boiler. Conversely, electric heating involves heating cables or mats installed underfloor, offering rapid warmth and easy installation, suitable for smaller areas. Both systems enhance comfort and energy efficiency, catering to diverse consumer preferences and building requirements in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
