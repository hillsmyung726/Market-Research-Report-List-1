<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Two-stage</li><li>Multi-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray drying equipment is categorized into single-stage, two-stage, and multi-stage systems based on operational complexity and efficiency. Single-stage systems involve a single drying chamber, ideal for straightforward applications. Two-stage systems separate the drying and classification processes, enhancing product quality and energy efficiency. Multi-stage systems utilize multiple drying phases to maximize moisture removal and optimize drying conditions, often suited for heat-sensitive materials. Each type caters to varying production scales and product specifications in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
