<p><strong>The Output Mode Cleaners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resonant Type</li><li>Non-resonant Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Output mode cleaners are devices used to enhance the performance of optical systems by filtering specific wavelengths of light. The resonant type utilizes resonant optical cavities to selectively transmit desired wavelengths while rejecting others, leading to high efficiency and narrow bandwidth. In contrast, non-resonant types rely on broader filtering mechanisms, using absorptive or reflective methods to achieve light cleaning over a wider range. Both types serve to improve signal quality in various applications, including telecommunications and laser systems.</p></p>
