<p><p>The truck and bus tire market is segmented into bias and radial tires. Bias tires, constructed with crisscrossed ply layers, offer durability and load-bearing capacity, ideal for rough terrains and heavy-duty applications. Radial tires, with perpendicular ply layers, provide better fuel efficiency, longer tread life, and improved handling, making them suitable for highways and urban use. While bias tires excel in off-road conditions, radial tires dominate on-road markets due to their performance advantages and cost-effectiveness over time.</p></p>
