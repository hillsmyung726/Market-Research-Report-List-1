<p><p>Folding ladders are categorized into four main market types based on material: aluminum, iron, fiberglass, and others. Iron ladders offer strength and durability, often used in industrial settings, but can be prone to corrosion. Fiberglass ladders are non-conductive and ideal for electrical work, ensuring safety around power sources. Other materials may include wood or advanced composites, catering to niche markets and specific use cases.</p></p>
