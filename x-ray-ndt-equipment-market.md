<p><p>X-Ray NDT (Non-Destructive Testing) equipment encompasses various technologies used for inspecting materials without causing damage. The three primary types are:</p><p>1. **CR (Computed Radiography)**: Utilizes phosphor plates to capture images, which are then processed digitally, offering flexibility and ease of use.</p><p>2. **DR (Digital Radiography)**: Employs digital sensors for real-time imaging, providing immediate results and high image quality.</p><p>3. **CT (Computed Tomography)**: Combines X-ray technology with advanced algorithms to create detailed 3D representations of objects, allowing for comprehensive internal analysis.</p></p>
