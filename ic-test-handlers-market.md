<p><strong>The IC Test Handlers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pick-And-Place Handlers</li><li>Gravity-Feed Handlers</li><li>Turret Handlers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC test handlers are crucial for testing integrated circuits efficiently. Pick-and-place handlers employ robotic arms to position devices for testing, offering high speed and flexibility for various package sizes. Gravity-feed handlers utilize gravity to align ICs for testing, providing a more straightforward and cost-effective solution, although generally slower. Turret handlers feature circular platforms that rotate to bring devices into test position, optimizing space and increasing throughput, making them suitable for high-volume production environments. Each type caters to specific testing needs.</p></p>
