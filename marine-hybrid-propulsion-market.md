<p><p>Marine hybrid propulsion refers to the integration of conventional internal combustion engines with electric propulsion systems, enabling vessels to operate on multiple power sources. This technology enhances fuel efficiency, reduces emissions, and provides operational flexibility, making it a sustainable solution for the maritime industry. Hybrid systems typically combine diesel engines, batteries, and electric motors, allowing ships to switch between power sources based on operational needs.</p><p>The Marine Hybrid Propulsion Market is experiencing steady growth, driven by increasing environmental regulations, rising fuel costs, and the demand for energy-efficient solutions.
