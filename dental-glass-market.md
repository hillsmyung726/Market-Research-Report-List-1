<p><strong>The Dental Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inert Glass</li><li>Reactive Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dental Glass Market includes Inert Glass and Reactive Glass types. Inert Glass is chemically stable, non-reactive, and primarily used for aesthetic restorations like veneers and crowns, offering durability and biocompatibility. Reactive Glass, on the other hand, chemically bonds with dental tissues, often used in bioactive materials for fillings or implants, promoting remineralization and enhancing tooth repair. Both types cater to different dental needs, with Inert Glass focusing on structural integrity and Reactive Glass emphasizing therapeutic benefits and integration with natural tissues.</p></p>
