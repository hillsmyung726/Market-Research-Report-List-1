<p><strong>The Absolute Pressure Transmitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AP Type</li><li>GP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Absolute Pressure Transmitters market is primarily categorized into two types: AP Type and GP Type. AP Type transmitters measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, making them ideal for applications requiring precise readings, such as in laboratories and environmental monitoring. GP Type transmitters, on the other hand, measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, commonly used in industrial applications where changes in atmospheric conditions may impact measurements. Both types are essential for maintaining accuracy in pressure measurement across various industries.</p></p>
