<p><p>Sputtering equipment cathodes play a crucial role in thin film deposition processes. The market can be divided into two main types: linear and circular cathodes. Linear cathodes offer a long, continuous target area, making them suitable for large substrates and high deposition rates. In contrast, circular cathodes provide a uniform deposition profile and are effective for smaller areas. Each type has unique advantages, catering to different applications in industries like electronics, optics, and coatings, ensuring efficient material utilization and performance.</p></p>
