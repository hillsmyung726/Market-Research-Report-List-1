<p><p>The food and beverage additives market includes several key types, each serving specific functions. Sweeteners enhance flavor, offering calorie-free or reduced-calorie options that cater to health-conscious consumers. Preservatives prolong shelf life by inhibiting microbial growth, ensuring food safety. Emulsifiers facilitate the blending of ingredients that typically don't mix, improving texture and stability in products like dressings and sauces. Other additives encompass a range of options, including colorants, flavor enhancers, and stabilizers, contributing to the overall quality and appeal of food and beverages.</p></p>
