<p><strong>The Hard Rock Shiled Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shielded Type TBMs</li><li>Open Type TBMs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hard Rock Shield Machine market encompasses two primary types of Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs): Shielded Type TBMs and Open Type TBMs. Shielded Type TBMs are designed with a protective shield that supports the tunnel face during excavation, ideal for unstable ground conditions, minimizing surface disruptions. In contrast, Open Type TBMs are utilized in more stable rock formations, allowing for direct excavation without a protective shield. Both types play essential roles in underground construction projects, catering to varying geological conditions.</p></p>
