<p><p>PTC heaters for household appliances include PTC air heaters, PTC heat conductors, and convection heaters. PTC air heaters efficiently warm air using positive temperature coefficient materials, providing quick heating for spaces. PTC heat conductors focus on transferring heat directly to surfaces or objects, offering targeted heating solutions. Convection heaters utilize the natural flow of heated air to circulate warmth in a room, making them suitable for whole-home heating. Together, these types enhance comfort and energy efficiency in households.</p></p>
