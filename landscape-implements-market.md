<p><strong>The Landscape Implements Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-tine</li><li>Rear-tine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Landscape implements can be categorized into front-tine and rear-tine tillers. Front-tine tillers, equipped with tines positioned at the front, provide better maneuverability and are ideal for small gardens or tight spaces. They are lightweight and easier to handle for residential users. In contrast, rear-tine tillers feature tines located at the back, which offer greater power and stability. These are typically used for larger plots and tougher soil conditions, making them suitable for more extensive landscaping tasks.</p></p>
