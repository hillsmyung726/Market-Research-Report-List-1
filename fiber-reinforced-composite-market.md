<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Composites</li><li>Thermosetting Composite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber reinforced composite market is primarily divided into thermoplastic and thermosetting composites. </p><p>Thermoplastic composites consist of polymers that can be reshaped upon heating, offering advantages like recyclability and ease of processing. They are commonly used in applications requiring high toughness and flexibility.</p><p>Thermosetting composites, on the other hand, are made of polymers that solidify permanently when cured. They provide superior mechanical properties and thermal resistance, making them ideal for high-performance applications in aerospace, automotive, and construction sectors.</p></p>
