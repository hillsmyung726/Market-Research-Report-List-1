<p><p>The Strong Acid Exchange Cation Resins market is primarily categorized into two types: Gel and Macroporous resins. Gel resins feature a uniform structure, offering high ion exchange capacity and are typically used for water softening and demineralization processes. In contrast, Macroporous resins have a more open structure, allowing for greater diffusion of larger ions, making them suitable for applications like waste treatment and selective ion removal. Both types play crucial roles in various industrial and environmental applications.</p></p>
