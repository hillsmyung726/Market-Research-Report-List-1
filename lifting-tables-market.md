<p><p>Lifting tables are categorized into electric, hydraulic, and pneumatic types based on their lifting mechanisms. Electric lifting tables utilize electric motors for smooth and precise height adjustments, making them ideal for industrial environments. Hydraulic lifting tables use fluid pressure for heavy lifting, offering robust performance in demanding tasks. Pneumatic lifting tables rely on compressed air, providing rapid and efficient movement, suitable for assembly lines. Each type serves unique applications, balancing power, speed, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
