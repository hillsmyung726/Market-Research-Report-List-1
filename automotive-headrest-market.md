<p><p>The automotive headrest market includes various types designed for comfort and safety. **Fixed headrests** are non-adjustable, integrated into the seat for basic support. **2-way headrests** allow vertical adjustment to align with the user's height. **4-way headrests** offer both vertical and horizontal adjustments for better positioning. **6-way headrests** provide the most flexibility, enabling vertical, horizontal, and angular adjustments for optimal comfort and safety.
