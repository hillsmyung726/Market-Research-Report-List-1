<p><p>Two-Dimensional Ultrasonic Wind Sensors are categorized based on the number of ultrasonic paths utilized to measure wind velocity and direction. In the Two Ultrasound Paths market, sensors use two ultrasonic transducers arranged in a specific configuration to determine wind parameters by analyzing the time of flight of sound waves between them. The Three Ultrasound Paths market employs an additional transducer, enhancing measurement accuracy and providing more comprehensive data on wind characteristics, enabling better performance in various applications.</p></p>
