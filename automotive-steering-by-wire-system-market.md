<p><p>The automotive steering-by-wire system market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Mechanical Steering (MS)**: Traditional systems using physical linkages for steering control.</p><p>2. **Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)**: Utilizes hydraulic pressure to assist in steering, enhancing driver ease.</p><p>3. **Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)**: Combines hydraulic systems with electric actuators for improved efficiency and responsiveness.</p><p>4. **Electric Power Steering (EPS)**: Fully electric system that uses electric motors to steer, offering adaptive feedback and energy savings. Each type reflects advancements in vehicle steering technology, improving control and comfort.</p></p>
