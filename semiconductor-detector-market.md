<p><p>The semiconductor detector market comprises various types catering to specific applications. Lithium drifting detectors enhance sensitivity and energy resolution for gamma-ray detection by utilizing lithium-drifted silicon. High purity germanium detectors are renowned for their exceptional energy resolution and are widely used in nuclear and gamma spectroscopy due to their ability to accurately measure gamma-ray energies. Each type serves unique roles in radiation detection and measurement.</p></p>
