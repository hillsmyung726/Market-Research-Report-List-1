<p><ul><li>Convection Oven</li><li>Vapour Phase Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SMD oven market includes various types, primarily convection ovens and vapor phase ovens. Convection ovens utilize forced air circulation to evenly distribute heat, ensuring efficient soldering of surface mount devices (SMDs) by maintaining consistent temperatures. Vapor phase ovens, on the other hand, employ vaporized liquid to create a uniform thermal environment, allowing for gentle yet effective heating. Both types are essential in electronics manufacturing for achieving reliable solder joints while minimizing thermal stress on components.</p></p>
