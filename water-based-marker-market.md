<p><p>The water-based marker market can be categorized into various types, primarily focusing on fiber and foaming types. Fiber type markers utilize porous tips made from synthetic or natural fibers, allowing for smooth ink flow and precise lines, popular in art and education. Foaming type markers feature foam tips that provide broader application, ideal for filling larger areas or creating textured effects. Both types leverage water-soluble inks, promoting easy cleanup and reducing harmful solvent emissions, making them environmentally friendly choices.</p></p>
