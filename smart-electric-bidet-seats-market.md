<p><p>Smart electric bidet seats come in three main types: tank type, tank-less type, and hybrid type. Tank type bidets feature a built-in reservoir for warm water, providing continuous use but taking up space. Tank-less types heat water on demand, ensuring a steady supply while being more compact and energy-efficient. Hybrid models combine both methods, offering the convenience of instant heating with the option for a reservoir. Each type caters to different user preferences for comfort, space, and efficiency.</p></p>
