<p><p>Crown moulding enhances interior aesthetics, available in various materials. **Polyurethane** moulding is lightweight, durable, and moisture-resistant, ideal for intricate designs. **Urethane** offers similar benefits with added flexibility, suitable for curved surfaces. **MDF** (Medium-Density Fiberboard) is cost-effective, paintable, and versatile but less durable in humid conditions. **Wood** moulding provides a classic, natural look but requires maintenance and is prone to warping. **Polystyrene** is budget-friendly and lightweight but less durable. **Others** include materials like PVC or plaster, catering to specific needs like moisture resistance or historical restoration.</p></p>
