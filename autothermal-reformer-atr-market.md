<p><p>An Autothermal Reformer (ATR) is a chemical reactor that converts hydrocarbons into syngas (a mixture of hydrogen and carbon monoxide) using a combination of steam and oxygen. The ATR market is segmented into non-desulfurization and desulfurization types. Non-desulfurization ATRs deal with feedstocks that contain low sulfur levels, while desulfurization ATRs are designed to handle feedstocks with higher sulfur content, utilizing additional processes to remove sulfur impurities. This differentiation impacts applications in industries like hydrogen production and gas-to-liquids technology.</p></p>
