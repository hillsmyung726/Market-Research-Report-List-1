<p><p>Mobile Application Management (MAM) solutions are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based MAM leverages remote servers for application management, offering scalability, flexibility, and ease of deployment, making it ideal for organizations looking to manage mobile applications without extensive infrastructure. On-premises MAM, on the other hand, involves hosting the management system within the organization’s facilities, allowing for greater control over data security and compliance, but often requiring significant initial investment and maintenance efforts.</p></p>
