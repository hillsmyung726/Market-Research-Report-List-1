<p><p>The diabetes drug market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Insulin**: Hormones essential for glucose regulation.  </p><p>2. **Sensitizers**: Improve insulin sensitivity in cells, enhancing glucose uptake.  </p><p>3. **SGLT-2 Inhibitors**: Prevent glucose reabsorption in kidneys, promoting excretion.  </p><p>4. **Alpha-glucosidase Inhibitors**: Delay carbohydrate absorption in the intestines, controlling blood sugar spikes.  </p><p>5. **Secretagogues**: Stimulate insulin secretion from pancreatic cells.  </p><p>6. **Peptide Analogs**: Mimic hormones like GLP-1, enhancing insulin release and reducing appetite.  </p><p>7. **Others**: Include various emerging therapies and combinations.</p></p>
