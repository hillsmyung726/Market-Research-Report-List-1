<p><p>Kidney dialysis equipment and supplies are essential for patients with renal failure, primarily categorized into two market types: hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis. Hemodialysis utilizes machines and artificial kidneys to filter blood through an external circuit, requiring supplies like dialyzers and bloodlines. In contrast, peritoneal dialysis involves instilling a sterile solution into the abdominal cavity, using catheters and dialysis fluid for waste removal. Both methods necessitate specific equipment and consumables to ensure effective and safe treatment for kidney disease patients.</p></p>
