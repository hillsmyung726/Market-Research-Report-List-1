<p><strong>The Digital Printing Sublimation Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Sublimation</li><li>Transfer Sublimation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital printing sublimation ink market is primarily divided into two types: direct sublimation and transfer sublimation. Direct sublimation involves printing designs directly onto polyester fabrics, allowing vibrant colors and detailed images suitable for apparel and promotional items. In contrast, transfer sublimation entails printing onto transfer paper first, which is then heat-pressed onto the substrate, enabling versatility in applying designs to a variety of materials, including ceramics and metals. Both types enhance product customization and visual appeal across various industries.</p></p>
