<p><p>Energy conservation service market types include BOT (Build-Operate-Transfer), EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction), EMC (Energy Management Contract), and EPC+C (EPC plus Continuous Services). In BOT, a private entity builds and operates a facility before transferring it to the government. EPC involves a contractor managing the design and construction phases. EMC is focused on energy efficiency improvements through a partnership that shares savings. EPC+C combines EPC services with ongoing management and optimization to ensure sustained energy efficiency.</p></p>
