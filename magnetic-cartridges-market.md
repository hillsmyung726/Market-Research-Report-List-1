<p><strong>The Magnetic Cartridges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Moving Magnet (MM)</li><li>Moving Coil (MC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic cartridges market primarily encompasses two types: Moving Magnet (MM) and Moving Coil (MC) cartridges. MM cartridges utilize a magnet attached to the stylus, allowing for simpler design and cost-effectiveness. They are favored for their warm sound and ease of use. Conversely, MC cartridges contain a coil fixed to the stylus, offering greater audio fidelity and sensitivity, but at a higher price and complexity in setup. Both types serve distinct preferences in the vinyl enthusiast community, influencing their market dynamics.</p></p>
