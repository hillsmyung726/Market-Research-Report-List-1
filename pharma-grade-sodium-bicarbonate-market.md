Fine grade sodium bicarbonate, characterized by its small particle size, is used in formulations requiring quick solubility and precise dosing, such as effervescent tablets and injectable solutions. Coarse grade, with larger particles, is often utilized in production processes that require bulk handling and slower dissolution rates, making it suitable for bulk pharmaceuticals and certain formulations. Both types are essential for quality control in pharmaceutical manufacturing.</p></p>
