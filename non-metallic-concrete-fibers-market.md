<p><p>The non-metallic concrete fibers market is primarily divided into macro-synthetic and micro-synthetic fibers. Macro-synthetic fibers are larger in diameter and provide structural reinforcement, enhancing tensile strength and crack resistance in concrete applications. They are typically used in industrial floors and shotcrete. In contrast, micro-synthetic fibers are smaller and primarily reduce plastic shrinkage cracking, improving overall durability. Both types serve to enhance the performance of concrete in various construction projects, increasing longevity and reducing maintenance costs.</p></p>
