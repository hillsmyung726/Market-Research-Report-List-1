<p><strong>The Oil and Gas Pipeline Leak Detection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mass-Volume Balance</li><li>Acoustic/Ultrasonic</li><li>Fiber Optics</li><li>Vapor Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Oil and Gas Pipeline Leak Detection market comprises several technologies. Mass-Volume Balance systems measure discrepancies between input and output volumes, indicating potential leaks. Acoustic/Ultrasonic methods utilize sound waves to detect abnormal changes in pipeline conditions. Fiber Optic sensors monitor temperature and strain along the pipeline, providing accurate leak detection through changes in light transmission. Vapor Sensing technologies detect gas emissions, alerting to leaks by monitoring gas concentrations around the pipeline. Each method offers distinct advantages for maintaining pipeline integrity.</p></p>
