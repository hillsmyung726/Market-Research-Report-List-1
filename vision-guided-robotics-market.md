<p><p>The Vision Guided Robotics market is categorized into two main types: 3D Vision Guided Robotics and Non-3D Vision Guided Robotics. </p><p>3D Vision Guided Robotics utilizes advanced imaging techniques to capture spatial information, enabling robots to perceive and navigate complex environments with precision. This technology is ideal for tasks requiring depth perception, such as assembly and inspection. </p><p>In contrast, Non-3D Vision Guided Robotics relies on 2D imaging, suitable for simpler applications like identification and pick-and-place tasks, where spatial depth is less critical.</p></p>
