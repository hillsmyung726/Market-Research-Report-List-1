<p><p>The switchgear installations market is categorized into three main types based on voltage levels: low voltage switchgear installations, medium voltage switchgear installations, and high voltage switchgear installations. Low voltage switchgear typically operates below 1 kV, controlling and protecting electrical circuits in residential and commercial settings. Medium voltage switchgear, ranging from 1 kV to 35 kV, is used in industrial and utility applications for distribution. High voltage switchgear operates above 35 kV, essential for transmission systems in power generation and distribution.</p></p>
