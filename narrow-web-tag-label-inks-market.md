<p><p>The Narrow Web Tag & Label Inks market features three primary types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are eco-friendly and suitable for a variety of substrates, offering quick drying times. Solvent-based inks provide excellent adhesion and durability, making them ideal for challenging surfaces. UV-cured inks utilize ultraviolet light for instant curing, resulting in high-quality, vibrant prints with strong resistance to fading and scratching. Each type caters to specific printing needs and sustainability considerations within the industry.</p></p>
