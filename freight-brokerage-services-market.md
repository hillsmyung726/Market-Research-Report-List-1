<p><p>Freight brokerage services encompass different market types, including truckload freight, LTL (less-than-truckload) freight, and others. Truckload freight involves full trucks dedicated to a single shipment, enhancing efficiency for large cargo. LTL freight combines multiple smaller shipments from various shippers into one truck, making it cost-effective for lighter loads. Other market types may include intermodal and specialized freight, catering to specific transportation needs. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing supply chain logistics and managing transportation costs.</p></p>
