<p><p>Polyphenylene Sulfone (PPSU) resins are available in two primary market types: powder PPSU and granular PPSU. Powder PPSU is typically used for applications requiring high thermal stability and chemical resistance, allowing for intricate molding processes. Granular PPSU, on the other hand, is often preferred for extrusion and injection molding, providing enhanced flow properties and easier processing. Both types are valued in industries like aerospace, automotive, and medical for their durability and performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
