Emulsion-based agents provide stability and a creamy texture, commonly used in frozen desserts and whipped toppings. Liquid whipping agents are easy to pour and mix, ideal for ready-to-use applications in both commercial and home kitchens. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
