<p><p>The continuous extrusion machine market is segmented into two main types: curvilinear and radial. Curvilinear continuous extrusion machines are designed for producing complex shapes with uniform cross-sections, often used in industries requiring intricate designs. On the other hand, radial continuous extrusion machines excel in producing cylindrical and tubular products, characterized by their efficient material flow and higher production rates. Both types cater to various applications across industries such as automotive, construction, and consumer goods, enhancing manufacturing efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
