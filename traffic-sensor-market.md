<p><p>The traffic sensor market comprises various technologies designed to monitor and manage traffic flow. Inductive loop sensors detect vehicle presence through electromagnetic induction, while piezoelectric sensors measure pressure changes. Bending plate sensors respond to vehicle weight, and image sensors utilize visual data for vehicle monitoring. Infrared sensors detect heat signatures, capturing traffic patterns in real-time. Additionally, "other" sensors include radar and acoustic systems, each offering unique advantages for traffic management and enhancing road safety.</p></p>
