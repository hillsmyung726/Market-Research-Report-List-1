<p><p>The Stretch Forming Machine market consists of two primary types: Sheet Stretch Forming Machines and Extrusion Stretch Forming Machines. Sheet Stretch Forming Machines are designed for shaping flat sheets of metal into complex forms by stretching and heating, making them ideal for producing aircraft panels and automotive components.
